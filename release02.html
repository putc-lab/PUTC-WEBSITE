Espedienti

    Espedienti is an informal quadraphony without preconditions. Four uninfluenced, rarefied chapters: do not correct. Do not refine. Do not beautify. Fiabe del fiume burns as slow-motion drift stuck in memory — sluggish loops, deflated synths, drunk guitars recorded at dawn, with the window open to the traffic outside. Liquida lingua is an exercise in rupture: slurred words, melted language over deliberately broken structures, an emulsion of off-key verses with no refrain. Incantesimi floats half-asleep in the blurred regions of a dreaming unconscious: from its amniotic suspension, details surface — faint outlines, snapshots soaked in soft melancholy. Eroinomadi is a diary written at the edges of hope: more musical in the classical sense, yet the most disjointed, dissolved, anesthetic: these are dreams transcribed too late, a navigation in search of what we manage to express — without maps, guided only by a sextant pointed straight at the most vulnerable and wordless core of our sensitive experience.

    Robert Bishop / Lilli composes and records using bare-bones tools and minimal tech. He moves along the borders of the song form — somewhere between lo-fi, hallucinatory trap, sound poetry, and linguistic derailments. His writing doesn't seek completion, but residue: like samples of dreams captured too late, his notes prefer the crack to the polished surface. Together with various characters, Bishop builds an intimate, faulty archive — different states of voice, memory, and noise. Vulnerable music, not made to please, but to remain.

    CARRO is a singer/songwriter who deconstructs languages and registers, blending them together with eclectic references to craft an original yet surreal sound.
